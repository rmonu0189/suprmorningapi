Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

SuprMorning values your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as phone number and profile information.
  • Address and delivery preferences needed to fulfill orders.
  • Transaction and order history for billing and support.
  • Device and app usage data for reliability and improvement.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process orders, subscriptions, and payments.
  • To provide support and account security.
  • To personalize catalog visibility and offers.
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted service providers required for operations, including payment, logistics, and communication partners.

4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to deliver services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

5. Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect user data. No method of transmission is fully risk-free.

6. Your Choices

You may request profile updates or account-related actions through the support channels available in the app.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes are reflected on this page with a revised date.

8. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us via support in the app.
