Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Welcome to SuprMorning. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website or use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: Financial details necessary to process wallet recharges and payments, processed securely through our payment partners.
  • Device & Usage Data: Information about your device, IP address, app interaction, and browsing history.
  • Location Data: Geolocation details to ensure accurate doorstep delivery of your daily essentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used to provide and enhance our services:

  • To process and deliver your orders every morning securely.
  • To manage your SuprMorning Wallet, subscriptions, and transactions.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, promotional offers, and service updates.
  • To improve our platform usage, bug fixes, and continuous service enhancement.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Delivery Partners: For the sole purpose of ensuring your order reaches your doorstep.
  • Service Providers: Payment gateways, cloud hosting services, and customer support infrastructure.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement rigorous security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. All payment details are encrypted using secure protocols and handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You can manage most of your data directly through the SuprMorning app, or by contacting our support team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please contact us at privacy@suprmorning.com.
