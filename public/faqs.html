Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Last updated: April 21, 2026

How do I place an order?

Select products, add to cart, choose your address and delivery slot, then complete payment.

Can I edit or cancel my order?

You can edit or cancel only within the allowed time window shown in your order details.

What payment methods are supported?

Available payment methods are shown at checkout and may vary by region and partner availability.

How can I track my order?

Go to your orders section in the app to view order status and recent updates.

What if an item is missing or damaged?

Report the issue through app support with your order ID for quick assistance.

How does subscription delivery work?

You can subscribe to eligible products and manage schedule or quantity from your subscription settings.

How do I contact support?

Use the support/help section in the app to reach our team.
